Profound, for a Sunday. (Nice of them to put April-Foolery Day on a weekend for once, wasn’t it? One of the ones on which I did wake before noon, but yes. Here’s the thing: I don’t frequent these websites, so I don’t know anything. For one thing, I don’t know if linking to this is like that album, “The Best of the Beatles”, is it? Old news by now. Say, did you know the one about the smell of burning coffee when you’re selling your house?
