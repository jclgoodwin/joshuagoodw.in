WrapperRhymes is a collection of poems written on wrappers. For example, Ted Hughes wrote on the sheath of a Tunnock’s Caramel Wafer in 1986:

To have swallowed a Crocodile
Would make anybody smile

But to swallow a Caramel Wafer
Is safer.

It scrolls endlessly; they are of varying quality.
