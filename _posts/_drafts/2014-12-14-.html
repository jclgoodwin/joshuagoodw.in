The internet site Twitter is abuzz with people “realising” the supposed fact that the numbers etched onto the dial on a toaster represent minutes (and not “the degree of toastiness” or some such).

I’m not sure what’s behind this trend, but it’s definitely a trend. There were 22 542 messages containing the words “toaster” and “minutes” posted on 2 December, compared to a typical 11 two days before. This one seems to have been the first:

so apparently the numbers on the toaster are minutes? ive thought for years it was degree of toasty-ness
